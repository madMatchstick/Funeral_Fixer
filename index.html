Last Minute Funeral Fixer

Providing meaning & fulfillment through music for any denomination during final farewells

Gabriel Silva

Ritual Space Music Specialist

I love music and performing above all else. The most profound moments of my musical journey have occurred while singing for final farewells. We all rely on music to honor our beloveds and those they leave behind. There is no thing more powerful than the memories we cherish of our departed loved ones. The power of music to fortify this bond between the living and the Ever-After is the power that gives me my own strength to face each day. I will humbly seek to connect the living to this bond should you ask me.

Services

This is an on-call service, which accommodates last minute bookings.

I provide the highest quality a cappella performances for your event.

I specialize in religious canons from all denominations.

I am able to learn and perform any song with or without accompaniment if given at least one hour to perfect it (four hours is necessary for songs in foreign languages).

Background

After receiving an Opera degree from UC Irving, I toured the opera houses of Europe. Back home, stateside, I found that my most fulfulling performances were for congregations of various religious denominations. Thus, I began to direct the music programs for Synogogues, Catholic and other Christian churches. It was during this time that I developed a deeper awareness of music in relation to ritual spaces.
